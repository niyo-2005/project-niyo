Our Story

Serving memorable meals since 2012

Saffron Table began as a small family dining room and grew into a modern restaurant known for seasonal produce, careful service, and reliable kitchen operations.

Our management system reflects that same culture: every order, table, customer, and inventory movement should be simple to track.
